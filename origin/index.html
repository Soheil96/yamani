Relationship to Prophet Muhammad (pbuh) and Ahl al-Bayt

Being Sayyed means, if you go up the fathers in ancestors, it ends up to Prophet Muhammad (pbuh) or one of his family (Ahl al-Bayt)

Ahmed claims to be Sayyed and uses that to assert being Yamaani and 1st Mahdi. His followers, known as Ansar al-Mahdi (/ænˈsɑːr ælˈmɑːhdi/) believe that Ahmed is Sayyed, and distribute a family tree for him referring to Imam Hojjat ibn al-Hasan (/ɪˈmɑːm hɔːˈdʒæt ɪbn ælˈhæsæn/), the 12th Imam of Shi'a muslims and going back to Imam Ali ibn-Abi Talib (peace be upon him).
