Biography of Ahmad Ismail al-Basri

1968

Ahmad Ismail was born in Basra, in the Huwair area in a small village called al-Madina.

Ahmad is from the Hambush tribe.

See more about his origin and lineage

1992

Ahmad received an engineering degree in urban planning from Basra University in Iraq.

1999

He entered the seminary of Mr. Sayyid Muhammad Sadr.

2002

He made his claim to be the Yemeni public.

2006

He goes into hiding.

2008

Ahmad and his followers launched an armed rebellion, and their intention was to kill the religious leaders of Najaf, especially Ayatollah Sistani, whom they considered an obstacle to the emergence of the Twelve Imam and an enemy.

2012

After four years of disappearance, a voice on a Facebook account claims to be Ahmad Basri.

2020

And he disappears again, until Eid al-Ghadir 2020, when he delivered a 17-minute sermon.
